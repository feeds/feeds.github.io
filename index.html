I am a doctoral student in computer science and physics at EPFL, in Lausanne, Switzerland. Mostly I collaborate with my wonderful lab mates at SPOC.

My current research focuses on gaining theoretical understanding of machine learning and combinatorial problems. How do complex phenomena emerge from small interactions? Some favourites: Cellular automata, opinion dynamcis, attention. To analyse them I use mostly statistical mechanics but also specific introspection techniques.

My background is in theoretical computer science, applied machine learning and software engineering. I also supervise students and I assist in teaching.
